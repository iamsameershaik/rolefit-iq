Product Data Analyst
Company: ShopMetric
Location: Manchester / Hybrid
Salary: £35,000 – £48,000

ABOUT THE ROLE
ShopMetric is a growing e-commerce analytics company looking for a Product Data Analyst to help product, marketing, and operations teams make better decisions. You will work across product usage, customer behaviour, conversion funnels, retention, and commercial performance.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Analyse product usage, conversion funnels, retention cohorts, and customer journeys.
- Write SQL queries to extract, join, clean, and validate data from PostgreSQL and BigQuery.
- Build dashboards in Power BI, Tableau, or Looker Studio for product and leadership teams.
- Investigate anomalies, performance changes, and experiment results.
- Translate ambiguous business questions into clear analytical approaches.
- Present insights and recommendations to non-technical stakeholders.
- Maintain metric definitions, dashboard documentation, and data quality checks.
- Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and marketing teams.

REQUIRED SKILLS
- Strong SQL skills.
- Experience with BI tools such as Power BI, Tableau, or Looker Studio.
- Experience analysing customer, product, sales, or marketing datasets.
- Good Python or R skills for data cleaning and exploratory analysis.
- Strong Excel or Google Sheets capability.
- Ability to explain findings clearly to non-technical audiences.
- Understanding of KPIs, funnels, cohorts, retention, and commercial metrics.

NICE TO HAVE
- Experience with e-commerce, SaaS, or marketplace analytics.
- dbt, Airflow, or data modelling experience.
- A/B testing or experimentation experience.
- Basic knowledge of product analytics tools such as Mixpanel, Amplitude, or GA4.
- Experience with dashboard UX and stakeholder training.

INTERVIEW FOCUS
We will ask candidates to walk through a dashboard or analysis they built, explain the data cleaning and metric definition process, and describe how their insight changed a business decision.
