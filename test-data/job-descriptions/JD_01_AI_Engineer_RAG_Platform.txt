AI Engineer — RAG Platform
Company: VectorWorks AI
Location: London / Hybrid
Salary: £55,000 – £75,000

ABOUT THE ROLE
VectorWorks AI is building retrieval-augmented AI products for professional services teams. We are hiring an AI Engineer to design and ship production-ready LLM systems that can ingest documents, retrieve relevant evidence, and generate grounded answers with citations.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Build document ingestion, chunking, embedding, and vector retrieval pipelines for uploaded PDFs, DOCX files, and text documents.
- Develop backend services in Python using FastAPI or similar frameworks.
- Integrate OpenAI, Anthropic, or equivalent LLM APIs into production workflows.
- Design prompts, structured outputs, evaluation datasets, and guardrails to reduce hallucination and improve trust.
- Build or support frontend interfaces in React and TypeScript for document upload, chat, citation display, and analysis dashboards.
- Implement logging, error handling, rate limits, and monitoring for AI workflows.
- Collaborate with product managers, designers, and customer-facing teams to understand user requirements.
- Document architecture decisions, model trade-offs, and known limitations.

REQUIRED SKILLS
- Strong Python engineering experience.
- Experience with LLM APIs, embeddings, RAG, vector databases, and prompt engineering.
- SQL and PostgreSQL experience.
- Familiarity with pgvector, Pinecone, Weaviate, or similar vector stores.
- Experience building APIs and integrating with modern frontend applications.
- Understanding of evaluation, grounding, citation quality, and responsible AI practices.
- Strong communication and ability to explain technical trade-offs.

NICE TO HAVE
- React and TypeScript experience.
- Supabase, serverless functions, or cloud deployment experience.
- Experience with LangChain, LlamaIndex, or custom orchestration.
- Docker, CI/CD, and observability tooling.
- Prior experience in legal, finance, consulting, or enterprise knowledge workflows.

INTERVIEW FOCUS
Candidates should be ready to discuss one AI system they built end-to-end, including data ingestion, chunking decisions, embedding choice, retrieval strategy, prompting, evaluation, failure modes, and productionisation trade-offs.
